What do we offer ?

Every organisation, including socially focused ones, can benefit from using data analysis to improve their practice. Whether it is to maximise donations, or to focus services to areas of most need. We offer a comprehensive range of support for social enterprises to utilise their data to improve outcomes.

We can help you:

Increase donations by effective targeting of investments.

  • Social media analysis for brand understanding.
  • Segment and target charity supporters for donations.
  • Churn analysis to try and identify people likely to stop donating.

Reduce costs by identify efficiency savings.

  • Analyse your operational data to highlight efficiencies.
  • Assist with determining the operational indicators to measure success.

Increase your positive impact through effective targeting of services.

  • Utilise your data as well as other big data and open data to determine were services are in most need.
  • Review key indicators to determine outcomes.

Empower you team to gain insights from your data to create knowledge to increase your impact.

  • Training in industry standard tools and analysis.
  • Consulting determine appropriate indicators for success.
  • Assisting with gathering, storing and analysing your data.
